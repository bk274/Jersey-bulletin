Product Detail

As a team, we made this application using skills and newer technologies learned in the class as part of our CS curriculum. In the back-end, we used popular programming language Python and its light-weight framework Flask to get up and running. In the front-end, we used another popular programming language Javascript and its library called React. To store your comments and be able to retain them for later use, we used postgresql a relational database management system. We used popular techology called git and github to let our team work on their part of the application on their own and still be able to collaborate successfully with other team members on this successful application for the people of New Jersey. To allow our fellow citizens to take advantage of our informative site, we deployed this on one of the most popular web hosting platform called Heroku.
We made this app for the purpose of giving New Jersey residents a place where they can find an agregation of all the information they need on a daily basis, all while communicating with others like them. This has the added benefit of helping to create a sense of community within the users of this app.
New Jersey is an eclectic state, with people usually too busy going about their day to participate in events designed to bolster their community. This app allows for users to calm down some of their daily chaos by only presenting the absolute necessities in an organized manner. This all with the added benefit of helping New Jersians feel more connected.
This site is very simple to use and it gives you important information to get started with your day in New Jersey. Click on the Home button at top of the page, this will take you to the main site where information like News, Traffic, and weather could be found. If you log in with your Google account after clicking that button on top, you will be given access to write comment on each forum under different tabs that will be seen by other users.
