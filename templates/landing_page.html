This box will include answer to how have you made it? As a team, we made this application using skills and newer technologies learned in the class as part of our CS curriculum. In the back-end, we used popular programming language Python and its light-weight framework Flask to get up and running. In the front-end, we used another popular programming language Javascript and its library called React. To store your comments and be able to retain them for later use, we used postgresql a relational database management system. We used popular techology called git and github to let our team work on their part of the application on their own and still be able to collaborate successfully with other team members on this successful application for the people of New Jersey. To allow our fellow citizens to take advantage of our informative site, we deployed this on one of the most popular web hosting platform called Heroku.

This box will include answer to question how to use this? This site is very simple to use and it gives you important information to get started with your day in New Jersey. Click on the Home button at top of the page, this will take you to the main site where information like News, Traffic, and weather could be found. If you log in with your Google account after clicking that button on top, you will be given access to write comment on each forum under different tabs that will be seen by other users.
